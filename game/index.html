TriTanks

Current Version: v1.8

Last Update: September 26th, 2023

Released December 25th, 2022.

How to Play:

WASD to move. Click to shoot.

Destroy enemy tanks.

Each tank color has a unique style. Try them all!

Press space to lay mines. You can lay a mine every 7 seconds.

Press F to open the chat. Press G to close the chat.

Remember, don't step onto mines that are not yours!

Any time you are killed, you will stay in your grave for 4.5 seconds.

Got suggestions? All ideas appreciated!



Tanks

Yellow: Good all around. Recommended for beginners.

Green: Fires two shots plus a slightly lower recoil and delay, but suffers from decreased accuracy and health.

Blue: Fires a long range, high damage, high velocity, and large sized bullet at the cost of high recoil and delay.

Red: Quickly fires low damage, range, and velocity bullets. Decreased recoil.

Purple: Decreased size makes it easy to dodge attacks, but is balanced by a weaker cannon. Noticably low spread.

Black: Shoots a volley of 8 extremely weak bullets at once. Use at short range.

Teal: Increased size and health. It uses a cannon that fires slightly faster and deals slightly less damage.

White: Increased delay, but higher damage/velocity/size bullets.

Pink: Although the Pink Tank has a very long delay, it shoots 10 shots at once. Similar to the Black Tank.

Orange: Yet another tank that fires multiple rounds. It fires 3 tight rounds at a long range.

Desert: Fires a high damaging shot at a slow rate. Great for sniping.

Ghillie: Extremely low health, speed, and fire rate, but you are basically invisible.

Mushroom: Extremely high fire rate, with a extremely weak bullet.
